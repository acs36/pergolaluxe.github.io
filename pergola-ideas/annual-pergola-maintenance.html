Annual Pergola Maintenance Checklist

A pergola is an outdoor structure typically consisting of vertical posts or pillars that support cross-beams and an open lattice, often covered with climbing plants. Unlike a fully enclosed gazebo, a pergola offers a balance between shade and sunlight, creating an inviting space for relaxation and outdoor gatherings.

To keep your pergola looking its best and to ensure its longevity, regular maintenance is crucial. An annual maintenance routine helps prevent damage from weather and wear, ensuring your pergola remains a safe and attractive focal point in your garden. This checklist will guide you through the essential steps to keep your pergola in top condition year after year.
